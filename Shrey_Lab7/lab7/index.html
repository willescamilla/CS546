About Mr.Chedda, the Cheeselord

Biography

Good evening fellow folk, I am Mr.Chedda but you shall refer to me as The Cheeselord, and nothing else. I was brought into this world to spread the beautiful word of the cheese and to bring forth the next generation of human beings. While harboring my fetus, my mother used to work in the concession stand at Wrigley field and everyday after her shift, she would drink all the extra nacho cheese as she was a humanitarian who hated wasting food, sending the nutritious nacho goodness straight into my tummy. My father is from Wisconsin so it can be argued that cheese flows through my veins.
