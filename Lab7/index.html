About Will Escamilla

Biography

I grew up on Long Island in a large family of Haitian and Colombian immigrants. Some of my relatives still lived in Queens or Brooklyn, but my parents moved out east to give me and my 2 older brothers a better education. Almost everyone in my extended family played soccer and they all are very smart. Since I'm one of the youngest, I've always had big shoes to fill!

The one thing that set me apart from my siblings and cousins was my love for video games. It led me down this haunting path of programming, and although finding interesting internships was tough, I have been able to accept a full-time offer at Amazon Games!
